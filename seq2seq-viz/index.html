SEQ2SEQ POLYLINE LAB

A tiny vanilla encoder–decoder RNN, trained live in your browser on hand-drawn polylines — with a self-attention decoder trained alongside a plain one, sharing the same encoder. Every point is a 3D vector [x, y, flag], where flag marks draw / break (new stroke) / stop (end of line), so the network learns not just where to draw but when to lift the pen or stop.
