Rollout / collapse tradeoff

Larger rollout (R) defends against collapse further into the future, but is harder to optimize — it usually needs a higher learning rate and more epochs. In practice the model only resists collapsing for roughly as many steps as it was trained to roll out.

Update equations

ht = act( Wxh·xt + Whh·ht-1 + bh )
ŷt = Why·ht + by
loss = mean( (ŷt − yt)² )
