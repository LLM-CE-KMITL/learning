Network Spec

Architecturedecoder-only Transformer
Attentioncausal, 2 heads
d_model16
Layers2
Output dim5 (dx,dy + 3 flag logits)
Every point attends back to every earlier point in the same stroke (causal mask — no peeking forward). One forward pass over a whole stroke computes the entire curriculum at once: position 1 predicts point 2, positions 1–2 predict point 3, and so on. Vectors are shown as (dx,dy,flag) — the first point of every stroke is (0,0,flag), later points are pixel deltas on a 300×300 canonical canvas, and flag ∈ {0=draw, 1=break, 2=stop}. The last point of a stroke is always trained as stop.
